About

I am passionate about creating digital products that work, are aesthetic, and meet user expectations. I have a background in visual communication and web design and strength in strategic problem solving. After writing my master's thesis on UX design I realized that a career in UX was the perfect way to merge my creativity, problem-solving skills, and desire for helping others.
